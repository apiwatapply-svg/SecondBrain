# Wiki As Codebase

The wiki can be treated like a codebase: markdown files are maintained artifacts,
links are dependencies, `AGENTS.md` is the schema, and git records changes.

## Practical Consequences

- Make small, reviewable edits.
- Keep naming conventions stable.
- Update indexes when files change.
- Use lint passes to find structural problems.
