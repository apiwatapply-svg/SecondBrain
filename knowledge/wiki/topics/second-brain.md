# Second Brain

A second brain is a personal knowledge system for capturing, organizing, and
reusing knowledge over time.

## In This Vault

This vault uses the LLM Wiki pattern so that the LLM does most of the linking,
summarizing, and maintenance work while the human curates sources and reviews
the result.
